Favorite Character: Hirono

Hirono is one of my favorite characters because of it's many expressions that is presented through out the Hirono series as well as the attention to detail that is presented in the character itself. The Hirono series are first created in 2021 by Chinese artist Lang for POP MART. The character was inspired by Lang's newborn child, his struggles upon moving to Beijing, and a desire to reflect the hidden emotions that are part of the human experience.
